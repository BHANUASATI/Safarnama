More About Safarnama

Welcome to Safarnama – my personal travel diary where I document my journeys across India. From the majestic mountains of Himachal to the serene beaches of Goa, every place has a unique story to tell.

Through my lens, I capture the beauty of each destination, sharing real experiences, stunning landscapes, and unforgettable moments.

  • Explored Places: Hidden gems, famous landmarks, and local favorites.
  • Travel Stories: Personal experiences, must-visit spots, and adventure tales.
  • Memories Captured: A collection of moments through my camera.
  • More to Come: The journey never stops!

Maharashtra

In 2021-2022, I visited Trimbakeshwar Jyotirlinga in Trimbak, Shani Shingnapur, Shirdi (Sai Baba Temple), Nashik, Brahmagiri Mountain, Ellora Caves, Aurangabad (Shri Grishneshwar Jyotirlinga), and the Godavari River origin. These sacred, historical, and scenic locations in Maharashtra offered spiritual experiences and breathtaking views.

Uttar-Pradesh

In 2022-2023, I explored the sacred cities of Mathura and Vrindavan in Uttar Pradesh, immersing myself in their spiritual richness. I visited the Krishna Janmabhoomi Temple in Mathura, where Lord Krishna was born, and also explored the Dwarkadhish Temple and Vishram Ghat. In Vrindavan, I experienced the divine aura of the Banke Bihari Temple, Prem Mandir, and ISKCON Temple, along with sacred places like Kesi Ghat and Radha Kund.

Madhya-Pradesh

In 2023-2024, I explored the famous places in Madhya Pradesh. I visited the bustling city of Indore, the sacred Omkareshwar and Maheshwar temples, and the historical Ujjain . I also explored Kundalpur, Bheemkund, and the spiritual sites of Jatasankar, Chitrakoot, and Maihar. The historic towns of Orchha and Dewas Tekri were other key highlights of my journey, along with the serene Bandakpur and the beautiful Indore city.

Rajasthan

In 2024-2025, I am planning a spiritual and cultural journey to Rajasthan. I will visit the famous Khatu Shyam Temple, known for its religious significance. I will also explore the vibrant city of Jaipur, home to iconic landmarks like the Amber Fort, Hawa Mahal, City Palace, and Jantar Mantar. Additionally, I plan to visit Salasar Balaji, a revered site dedicated to Lord Hanuman, adding a spiritual touch to my travel experience.
